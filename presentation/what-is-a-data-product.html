What is a Data Product?

It's More Than Just a Table in a Database.

What it is NOT:
Data Exhaust

This is our current reality. Data is a passive byproduct of our operational systems. It's the "exhaust" from our running applications.

Siloed & Hidden

Inconsistent & Untrusted

No Clear Owner

Result: High Costs, Slow Decisions, Compliance Risks.

What it IS:
A Trusted, Reusable Asset

A Data Product is an asset that has been intentionally designed, built, and managed for consumption. It's our "finished good."

Discoverable

Trustworthy

Secure & Governed

Owned

Result: Faster Innovation, Higher Quality Insights, Automated Governance.
